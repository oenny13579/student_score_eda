### Student Exam Scores EDA

#### Objective: 
Conduct a simple EDA to identify factors that may affect students exam results such as attendance, sleep hours and hours studied.

#### Findings:
1. 'sleep_hours' and 'hours_studied' affects the student score positively.
2. 'attendance_percent' has not much relationship with the student score.

Data retrieved from: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/mirzayasirabdullah07/student-exam-scores-dataset
